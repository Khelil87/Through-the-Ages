Project Name



The story is, that you start off as a caveman amongst small tribe/village. You, however are the next level of intelligence and your forward thinking and crazy ideas get you banished from the tribe. From here your goal is to start a new tribe as their leader all while surviving the elements, environment, and eventually hostile tribes. From here, you will advance to the “New Ages” as our name would imply.
